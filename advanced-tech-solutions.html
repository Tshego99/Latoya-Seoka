Robotic Process Automation (RPA)


What is Robotic Process Automation (RPA)?

RPA is a division in LOR Technologies which is a dedicated to focus on Robotic Process Automation (RPA) and refers to the use of software robots or digital workers to automate repetitive, rule-based tasks that are typically performed by humans. RPA tools can mimic human actions, such as clicks, keystrokes, and data entry, to execute processes more quickly, accurately, and efficiently. The development of bespoke RPA automation solutions entails creating custom automations specifically designed to address an organization’s unique requirements related to accounting, data management, system administration, reading, and processing tasks. Some key components of RPA development include:

  • Process identification
  • Process mapping:
  • Tool selection and customization
  • Development of automations
  • Testing and validation
  • Deployment and monitoring
  • Maintenance and continuous improvement

In summary, RPA involves the use of software robots to automate repetitive, rule-based tasks across different functions within an organization. Bespoke RPA solutions specifically cater to an organization’s unique requirements, such as in accounting or data management, and involve a systematic process of identification, mapping, development, testing, deployment, and continuous improvement to ensure the automation delivers the desired results.
